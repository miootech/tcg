♠️ Texas Hold'em — Rules

🎯 Objective

Build the best 5-card hand from your 2 Hole Cards and up to 5 Community Cards. Best hand wins the pot.

🔄 Game Flow

1. Pre-Flop — 2 hidden Hole Cards each. SB (50🔴) & BB (100🔴) post blinds. UTG acts first.

2. Flop — 3 Community Cards revealed. Betting round starts left of Dealer.

3. Turn — 4th Community Card. Another betting round.

4. River — 5th Community Card. Final betting → Showdown.

🃏 Actions

Fold — Give up. Lose current bet.  |  Check — Pass (no open bet).

Call — Match the bet.  |  Raise — Increase (min = cur + 100).  |  All In — All chips.

🏆 Hand Ranking (Strongest → Weakest)

🥇 Royal Flush — A K Q J 10 same suit
🥈 Straight Flush — 5 consecutive, same suit
🥉 Four of a Kind — 4 identical values
4️⃣ Full House — Three of a Kind + Pair
5️⃣ Flush — 5 cards same suit
6️⃣ Straight — 5 consecutive values
7️⃣ Three of a Kind — 3 identical values
8️⃣ Two Pair — Two different pairs
9️⃣ One Pair — One pair
🔟 High Card — Highest card decides

🎰 Blinds & Buy-In

SB: 🔴 50 · BB: 🔴 100 · Buy-in: 🔴 up to 2,000 chips from your balance.
